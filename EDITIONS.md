# American Men (and Women) of Science — Editions

Links to publicly available access to different editions. PDFs are available for download up to the 4th edition (1927).

## Pre-WWII: Single-Volume Editions

- 1st Edition (1906): ~4,000 scientists — https://ia800805.us.archive.org/32/items/americanmenofsci01catt/americanmenofsci01catt_bw.pdf
- 2nd Edition (1910): ~5,500 scientists — https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015039431930&seq=15
- 3rd Edition (1921): ~9,500 scientists — https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015068230864&seq=7
- **4th Edition (1927): ~13,500 scientists — https://babel.hathitrust.org/cgi/pt?id=mdp.39015039431948&seq=7** *(this pipeline)*
- 5th Edition (1933): ~22,000 scientists — https://archive.org/details/bwb_S0-DTZ-242/page/n5/mode/2up
- 6th Edition (1938): ~28,000 scientists — https://archive.org/details/bwb_S0-EDA-828/page/n3/mode/2up
- 8th Edition (1949): ~50,000 scientists — https://archive.org/details/bwb_S0-CGR-608/page/n7/mode/2up

## Post-WWII: Multi-Volume Editions

- 9th Edition (1955–1956): split into Physical/Biological and Social/Behavioral
- 11th Edition (1965–1970): ~160,000 scientists (8 volumes + supplements)
- 13th Edition (1976–1978): title changed to *American Men and Women of Science*
- 19th Edition (1995–1996): 8 volumes
- 38th Edition (2020): 18 volumes
